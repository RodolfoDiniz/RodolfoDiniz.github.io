Apple's WWDC Scholarship Program enables students and new developers to request the chance to attend the conference with most of the expenses covered.

As my first year applying for the scholarship, I spent 10 days at the Apple Developer Academy busting my head to create an experiment to submit to the WWDC19. And from there was born CUBX, a puzzle carefully made for players who like the genre - or for those who need some moments of visual tranquility.

Among several creative projects I was one of the award-winning students to go to the US to spend a week in San Jose/CA taking advantage of every second of the lectures and sessions provided by Apple.
