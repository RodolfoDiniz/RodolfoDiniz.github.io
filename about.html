In 2012 I moved to Curitiba to start a higher education. Like every young person at this age, I was completely lost as to what career path to pursue and eventually I choose to study Law at PUCPR.

During my degree I worked in some places, and even at Walt Disney World, which was an amazing experience where I learned a lot about business - especially with customer service (which will be very important in the future of this timeline) - but none of those other jobs suited me. Until I joined a law firm, Efing & Rocha, where I spent almost two years there, I was very fond of my work because I worked with the academic area. But I still couldn't see myself realized as a professional in the area I was working.

So I realized that I had to go on a path to find my true motivation. I noticed that all my hobbies and passions were related to an area that I had never even considered before, Design. And going to lectures and taking courses I found myself falling more and more in love with this area full of creativity and innovation. So I started a new degree, this time in Design at PUCPR, where I could choose from a variety of elective courses and focus in the Interaction Design.

Later I left the law firm to look for opportunities in the new area, and some time later I joined the Apple Developer Academy. It has been a unique and very rewarding opportunity since I’m learning a lot about user interface and developing coding skills.
