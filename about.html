Technology enthusiast and passionate about Design. I like to work on ambitious projects and with people who are passionate about what they do.

In 2012, I moved to Curitiba to start Higher Education. Like almost every young person under 20, I was undecided as to what career path to pursue, and eventually, I chose to study Law.

During my five-year degree, I worked in the public area, in Law offices, but none of these jobs suited me. Within this period, I had a three-month work experience at Walt Disney World Resort, which was tremendous. Over there, I learned a lot about business - especially about customer service, which has inspired me to build my customer service philosophy and skills to my current working area.

After finishing my degree in Law, I realized that I had to get into my inherent interests to find my self-motivation. I noticed that my hobbies and passions were concerned with an area that I had never even considered before - Design. After going to lectures and taking courses, I found purpose and meaning in this fantastic area full of creativity and innovation. So, I started my new degree, this time in Design, where I could choose from a variety of elective courses and focus on the area of User Experience and User Interaction.

Currently I am part of the Apple Developer Academy. It has been a unique and very rewarding opportunity since I have been learning a lot about design and developing coding skills.
