Juicy Way is a galactic game that is set on a fruity galaxy. It is a free endless runner 2D experience developed by one designer and one developer.

Juicy Way was born in a three weeks challenge at the Apple Developer Academy, in which we had to develop and publish a hyper casual game with the aim of applying freemium monetization and monitoring the results.

Some general parameters that we had: hyper-casual game; preferably 2D with few visual elements and interactions; rapid phases/interactions lasting from ten seconds to two minutes.

In the first week we developed the basic mechanics and submitted to Test Flight; in the second week we included monetization and analytics, and submitted for publication; on the third and final week the focus was on user acquisition, campaign development and metrics tracking.

The app is in it's first version and you can download it by clicking here.
